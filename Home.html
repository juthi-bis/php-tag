EXPERIENCE THE BEAUTY OF THE BAY AND HILL TRACTS FROM THIS CHITTAGONG CITY HOTEL

As the first upper upscale international accommodation in Chittagong City, the Radisson Blu Chittagong Bay View offers a modern lodging experience within walking distance M.A. Aziz Stadium and Chittagong Club Limited. Convenient amenities like free high-speed, wireless Internet, a Business Class Lounge and Chittagong’s largest pillarless event facility allow business travelers to stay productive during their stay. When you're winding down from a full day of activity, let the stress melt away with a swim in our circular infinity pool, a beverage from a pool bar or a visit to the hotel spa. After enjoying a restful night’s sleep in the contemporary furnishings of your room or suite, start your morning with a complimentary breakfast. Later in the day, savor international cuisine, freshly pressed juices and classic cocktails found in our eight different restaurants and bars.

View

This property treats guests to breathtaking views of the Hill Tracts and Chittagong Bay.

Local Attractions

Just a 10-minute drive from the city’s business hub and 40 minutes from Shah Amanat International Airport (CGP), our hotel is also within walking distance of M.A. Aziz Stadium and Batali Hill.

Inviting Rooms

Relax in the upscale comfort of our 241 rooms, which feature amenities like satellite television, mini bars, memory foam beds and plush down bedding.
